🛒 Walmart Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Walmart sales performance across years, categories, cities, and delivery status, helping stakeholders gain actionable business insights.

📌 Project Overview

This project visualizes Walmart’s sales data to track revenue, profit, quantity sold, and category-wise performance.
The dashboard enables users to quickly identify:

Sales and profit trends over time

High-performing and low-performing product categories

Overall business KPIs at a glance

Impact of delivery status and city-level filtering

🎯 Key Objectives

Analyze year-wise sales growth

Identify top revenue and profit-generating categories

Monitor overall sales, profit, and quantity

Provide interactive filters for better decision-making
